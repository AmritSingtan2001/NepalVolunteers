Why Choose Nepal Volunteers

A Nepal Volunteers we have a successful way to keep our projects running. We are happy to be a sustainable, professional and local organization that takes good care or our projects, community and volunteers.

Local & Sustainable

We are a local organization: part of your volunteering and trekking fee goes to the projects.

Fair & Affordable Fees

We are transparent in our fee system, have low overhead costs and still offer great service.

Experienced Support

We have a great local support team that takes care of you 24 hours per day when needed.

Preparation is Key

We train you to be a great volunteer: we teach you first about our language and culture.

Home Aaway from Home

We have fun, loving and well-trained host families that you will spend your time with.

Protection Policies

We care for your safety and the safety of the people in our communities and projects.
