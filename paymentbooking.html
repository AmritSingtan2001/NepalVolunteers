Payment And Booking Process


Program Fee

  1. Registration & Processing Fee - One Time & Nonrefundable Registration & Processing Fee - Euro $ 100
  2. .Monthly Program Fee - Euro $ 200 Per month ( It includes all food , Accommodation with shared bathroom & Kitchen and shared room with separate bed ,free Wi-Fi Internet , Electricity , water ,cleaning and waste management )
If you will need a private bedroom without shared then extra 50 Euro per month shall be applied
Note - The Program Fee is monthly based because all the major expenses are in monthly agreement so the minimum Program fee is at least 1 month no matter if you may choose to stay 1 week or 2 or less than 1 month.
Any services or facilities which are not included shall be your own responsibilities.
Accommodation - We have our own & our Members Family Farmers Homestay Houses almost in every Part of Nepal with Nepalese Normal single bedroom with clean blanket, bedsheet, pillow, mattress, and common bathroom with shower, Wi Fi internet, Electricity.
If you need more Luxurious than this you have freedom to add but with your own money.

Food

  1. Tea & breakfast - Organic Green Tea /black tea and fresh Milk tea with Nepalese typical breakfast such as boiled egg potatoes ,bread chapatti Bread ,chiura ,soup
  2. Lunch - Daal bhat sabji - Veg and Non Veg Lunch shall be provided with Nepalese typical food
  3. Dinner - Nepalese typical daal bhat (Lentil Soup & Steam Rice) with veg or non veg shall be provided.

If you need more than this you have full freedom to buy on your own Visa & Others

Visa and others are your own responsibility .We shall assist you with the documentation and processing wherever needed
