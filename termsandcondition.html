  • Illegal or violent behaviour shall result in immediate termination from the program.
  • Volunteers shall not get involved in any kind of proselytising, evangelising and political affairs apart from illegal activities.
  • Volunteers are strictly prohibited to bring any book magazines, CDs, DVDs that are inappropriate for anyone in Nepal. The use of narcotic drugs is sternly prohibited.
  • Always keep a photocopy of your passport with you. Donâ€™t pass your telephone number, email address to any stranger.
