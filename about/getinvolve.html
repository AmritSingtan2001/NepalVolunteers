Get Involved


Make an impact: There is more than one way

Join us and make an impact! Help bring about transformation in Nepal. As a charitable organisation, we are dependent on the kindness and generosity of our volunteers and donors to fund our projects. We are thankful for those who have supported the organisation over the years. You help Nepal Volunteers to make changes to the lives of thousands of underprivileged children and women in Nepal. We need motivated, passionate individuals to raise awareness and funds for our work. Besides volunteering, there are many ways to get involved.


1. Partner with us

Nepal Volunteers works with many local and international partners. We work with volunteer sending agencies, small businesses, universities, schools and community organisations. We are always looking for partner organisations that can help us on our road to sustainability.Nepal Volunteers understands that truly effective partnerships only occur when the partnership benefits both parties. We want partners that can align themselves with our values and vision. VSN believes that poverty can be defeated, that all children deserve an education. And that investing in leaders and community will create generational change, and that everyone deserves a life of dignity.


2. Support a Family Farmer/ Youth & Women Entrepreneurs

Nepal Volunteers is currently providing training ,skill development , capacity building to Family Farmers , Youth & Women Entrepreneurs . We do not receive financial support/help from the government. We receive only private donations, together with the assistance from its volunteers.

# Sponsor Children Education - In order for Nepal Volunteers to expand and reach out to more children it needs your generous support. You may help in any of the following ways:

  • If you desire to sponsor a Child Education with his/her full sponsorship paying for his/her annual charge you could pay 1000 euro per year per child. Nepal Volunteers will send you a photo, a brief history, and an annual report on your child.
  • You can also donate to the general fund in any amount. The general fund provides food, clothing, shelter, medical and dental care, legal services, and quality education to the children.



3. Join our Tours and Treks

Nepal Volunteers is also a registered CBT - Community Based Tourism in Nepal . We are called EcoHomeStay Nepal . Our goal is to make a difference through Resilience ,Sustainable , Responsible Community Based Tourism . Being responsible for maintaining the environment, its people and culture is an important factor to consider when travelling abroad. We not only offer services that are ethical and thoughtful, but also offer opportunities for travellers to use their time and money to make a difference.

We arrange a wide range of trekking, tours, rafting and jungle safari. We also offer great combinations of trekking and volunteering, or touring and volunteering. During the time with us, we will share our knowledge of the history of this vast region, as we explore villages, trails, cities, palaces and temples. Our trekking, tour and travelling boosts our sustainability to continue the humanitarian works in Nepal. We support our Women Development Project and After School Program to serve underprivileged women and children in Nepal.

4. Donate / Sponsor /Support

Our projects would not be possible without the help of our donors. Every little contribution is appreciated. A contribution can go a long way. for example by helping provide essential care and supply our schools. Or it gives an opportunity for women to learn and to help build our communities. If you wish to donate please contact us and we can discuss how your contributions can help.
